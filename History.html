A-class

The Mercedes-AMG A 35 and A 45 S Hatchback not only have “AMG” in their names, but also under the bonnet. The new belt-driven starter-generator (RSG) with 48 V onboard system redefines performance for the AMG A-Class. The key to superior performance is the optional AMG RIDE CONTROL suspension. The adaptive damping offers a wide spread between comfortable and very sporty setup. You choose between Comfort, Sport or Sport+ with just one operation and the suspension characteristics change instantly.


The incomparable driving pleasure is taken to the next level by the new 8-speed automatic transmission. With the driving programme, you also change the characteristics of the dual-clutch transmission. It uses the 8 gears either for efficient driving at low revs or for impressive dynamics.

GLE-class

The Mercedes-AMG GLE Coupé offers power in every situation. This is made possible by genuine masterpieces of engineering. Under the bonnet of the Mercedes-AMG GLE 53 4MATIC+ SUV is a 3.0-litre R6 turbo engine that makes a clear performance statement. This is only surpassed by the 4.0-litre V8 biturbo engine in the Mercedes-AMG GLE 63S 4MATIC Coupé. The new models, which are manufactured according to the "one man, one engine" principle, now receive additional power from the 48-volt electrical system - for an even livelier driving experience.

GT-class

Overtake everything that has come before: the Mercedes-AMG GT 63 S E PERFORMANCE is the most powerful production vehicle we have ever made. Highly efficient hybrid technology meets our unmistakeably sporty DNA. The result: More AMG than ever before.

C-class

Incredible performance meets boundless passion: The Mercedes-AMG C 63 S E PERFORMANCE is the uncompromising combination of power, luxury, efficiency – and pioneering technology. For pure emotions. And the pure AMG experience.

E-class

The updated exterior design is reflected in the completely redesigned front end with its AMG-specific radiator grill featuring twelve vertical slats and the enlarged central star. The rear design conveys more elegance and lightness with its new, flatter and now two-part taillights. The newly designed rear spoiler emphasizes the elegant lines and also has a positive effect on the vehicle’s aerodynamics.The exterior’s front and rear spoiler as well as light-alloy wheels and exhaust system are also perfected in the new model. The MANUFAKTUR brilliant blue magno paint finish available exclusively for the Mercedes-AMG E 63 4MATIC+ and E 63 S 4MATIC+ is fascination pure.
